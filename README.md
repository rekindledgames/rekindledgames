## RekindledGames 🔥

Indie game studio and Unity asset publisher.

We build **RekindledFX** — procedural VFX for **Unity URP**: electricity, frost & ice,
lava, and volumetric light beams. Zero textures, zero dependencies, just drop them in.
We're also developing our own game, **Diaspera**.

### RekindledFX — Procedural VFX for Unity
- ⚡ **Lightning** — procedural electric arcs
- ❄️ **Frost & Ice** — freeze, frost coat & shatter
- 🌋 **Lava** — flowing lava shader + rising embers
- 🔦 **Volumetric Light Beam** — god rays & volumetric beams
